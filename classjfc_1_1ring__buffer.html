template<class value_type_template, size_t size_template>
class jfc::ring_buffer< value_type_template, size_template >

fixed length, contiguous, linear collection with iterators that wrap around its lower and upper limits rather than go out of bounds

to access the internal data, use get to construct an iterator to the data or cget to construct a const_iterator to the data
